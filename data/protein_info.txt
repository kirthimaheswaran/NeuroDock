Target Protein: Acetylcholinesterase (AChE)

PDB ID: 4EY7

Disease:
Alzheimer's Disease

Function:
Acetylcholinesterase is an enzyme that breaks down the neurotransmitter acetylcholine.
In Alzheimer's disease, increasing acetylcholine levels can improve memory and cognitive function.

Why this target?
Many Alzheimer's drugs such as Donepezil inhibit acetylcholinesterase.
Consensus docking helps identify molecules that may bind strongly to this enzyme.
